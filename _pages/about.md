Hi!

I'm an Associate Teaching Professor of Government at William & Mary. I'm also a Faculty Affiliate in the International Relations, Public Policy, and Latin American & Caribbean Studies programs.

I teach courses on Latin American politics, political regimes, organized crime, research methods, and data visualization. My research examines authoritarian legacies and democratic backsliding in Latin America. Please visit my Teaching and Research pages for more information.

Before arriving at William & Mary, I earned my Ph.D. in Political Science at the University of Chicago.
